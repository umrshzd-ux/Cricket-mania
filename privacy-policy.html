Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 01, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Cricket Mania ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to be played offline, and we collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Game progress (scores, coins, level, XP), unlocked items (bats, stadiums, cosmetics), achievement progress, mission progress, settings preferences (sound, music, difficulty), and daily streak status are stored only on your device using localStorage and are never transmitted to our servers.

2.2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party advertising services that may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID, IDFA), IP address, device type and operating system, app usage data, and coarse location (country-level only). This is used to serve advertisements by:

  • Google AdMob (Privacy Policy)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for displaying advertisements, improving app performance, and complying with legal obligations. We do NOT sell your personal data, collect your name or contact details, track your precise location, or access your contacts, photos, or other device content.

4. Advertising & Consent

For users in the EEA and UK, you have the right to choose between personalised or non-personalised ads. A consent dialog will appear when you first launch the app. You can change your consent anytime in the app settings or by resetting your advertising ID in your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or opt-out of data collection. Uninstall the app or clear app storage to delete your data. Decline personalised advertising through the consent dialog to opt-out of personalised ads.

8. Third-Party Links

Our app does not contain links to external websites or services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here and on our app store listing.
